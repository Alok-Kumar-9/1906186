BIHAR

Bihar is a state in eastern India. It is the third-largest state by population and twelfth-largest by territory, with an area of 94,163 km2 (36,357 sq mi). It is contiguous with Uttar Pradesh to its west, Nepal to the north, the northern part of West Bengal to the east, and with Jharkhand to the south.


History of BIHAR


In ancient and classical India, the area that is now Bihar was considered a centre of power, learning, and culture.From Magadha arose India's first empire, the Maurya empire, as well as one of the world's most widely adhered-to religions: Buddhism.


Cultue and Language of BIHAR


The Bihar plain is split by the river Ganges, which flows from west to east.Three main cultural regions converge in the state: Magadh, Mithila, and Bhojpur.Bihar is also the world's third-most populous subnational entity.
